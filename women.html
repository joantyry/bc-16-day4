Believe in yourself, you can make and nothing should shutter away your dream. Another thing that hurts is this group of ladies who believe men should do it, and never make any effort to become better. They just sit around and wait for manna to drop from heaven. Some of them bring other women down who strive for excellence. Wake up its never too late to achieve your dreams. Go for it, own it.
